# Travel Help

## Baggage policy
Domestic flight cabin baggage is typically one small bag and one personal item. Check final airline ticket rules.

## Check-in guidance
Web check-in usually opens 24 to 48 hours before departure. Keep ID and booking reference ready.

## Visa requirements
Visa rules depend on destination country, passport, and travel purpose. Verify with official embassy sources before travel.

## Passport validity
Many destinations require passport validity of at least 6 months from travel date.

## Travel insurance
Insurance can cover cancellation, medical emergencies, and baggage loss. Compare plans before booking.

## Insurance claim basics
Keep invoices, medical reports, police reports (if needed), and boarding passes to file claims quickly.

## Cancel booking
Provide booking id and confirm cancellation request.

## Cancellation charges
Charges depend on fare type and cancellation time. Partial refunds may apply for non-refundable fares.

## Itinerary
Ask: Show my itinerary to retrieve booking details.

## Reschedule trip
Date change is subject to seat availability and fare difference. Additional airline fees may apply.

## Airport reporting time
Report at least 2 hours before domestic and 3 hours before international departures.
