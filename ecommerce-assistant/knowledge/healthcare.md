# Healthcare Help

## Visiting hours
Standard visiting hours are 5 PM to 7 PM daily unless restricted by ward policy.

## Emergency care
Emergency and trauma services are available 24x7. Critical patients are prioritized by triage severity.

## Admission documents
Bring ID proof, insurance card if available, prior prescriptions, and recent medical reports.

## Insurance and TPA desk
For cashless treatment, submit insurance details early at the helpdesk for pre-authorization.

## Test preparation
Follow fasting instructions when required. Carry previous reports and arrive 15 minutes early.

## Lab report turnaround
Routine blood tests are generally available same day, while specialized tests may take longer.

## Book appointment
Provide doctor name and preferred date or slot.

## Teleconsultation
Video consultations are available for follow-up in selected departments.

## Cancel or reschedule
Provide appointment id and updated preferred date/time.

## Pharmacy timings
In-house pharmacy is usually open from early morning till late night; emergency counter may run 24x7.
